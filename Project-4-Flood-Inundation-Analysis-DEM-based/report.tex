\section*{Objective}
This project analyzes flood inundation using synthetic DEM data and rising water levels.
\section*{Workflow}
The pipeline generates a 100 by 100 DEM, calculates flooded cells and inundation depth, visualizes flood extent at multiple water levels, and evaluates flood-area growth from 40 m to 50 m.

\section*{Validation}
The validation suite tests 101 water levels and confirms all 506 physical checks pass, including non-negative depths, correct maximum depth, and monotonic flood-area growth.
